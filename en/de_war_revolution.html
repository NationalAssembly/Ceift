War and Revolution (1914–1918)

During the First World War, Germany imposed military law and censorship. The war gradually culminated in economic collapse and mass strikes for peace. Germany's defeat led to revolution, the abdication of Emperor Wilhelm II, and the proclamation of the German Republic in November 1918.
