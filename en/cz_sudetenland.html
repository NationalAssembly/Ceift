Sudetenland and End of Republic (1938–1939)

The rise of Adolf Hitler in Germany led to increasing pressure on Czechoslovakia due to its German-speaking population in the Sudetenland. The Munich Agreement in September 1938 forced Czechoslovakia to cede these territories. The following year, the remaining Czech and Moravian territories fell under German occupation, ending the First Czechoslovak Republic.
