Weimar Revolution (1918–1919)

Immediately after the war, Germany faced revolutionary unrest. The Spartacist uprising was suppressed, but it led to the adoption of an ambitious Weimar Constitution, which introduced universal suffrage including women and new forms of worker representation through works councils.
