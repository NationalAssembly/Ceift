First Republic — Building (1918–1923)

In its early years, Czechoslovakia faced border disputes with neighboring states while building the foundations of a democratic nation domestically. A final constitution was adopted that guaranteed equal rights for women in political participation, the first parliamentary elections were held, and measures were adopted to protect the new democratic system.
