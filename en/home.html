CENTRAL EUROPE INTERWAR FREEDOM TIMELINE

Discover the history of freedom in Central Europe after World War I through an interactive timeline and articles!

About the Project

CEIFT was created as part of the Czech-German student project Svodoba–Freiheit–Freedom between the Prague Gymnasium Jana Keplera and the Dresden Gymnasium Bürgerwiese.

CEIFT aims to document the history of freedom in Central Europe during the interwar period, using the heart of the website – an interactive timeline with German events at the top and Czechoslovak events at the bottom. From the events on the timeline, links lead to short thematic articles supplemented with additional historical materials. The articles are complemented by a podcast about the Weimar National Assembly, placed below.

About the Website

Note: The website is under development. Much of the content currently contains placeholder text and images or is missing entirely. Errors may occur.

The website is multilingual, with language options available in the top right corner. Next to that is the ability to change cursors! For navigation between articles, you can use the timeline or the top menu.

We share the entire website and our original content under open licenses! More information below in the footer.

The website uses free hosting on the GitHub Pages platform. It uses no cookies or external analytics and fully respects user privacy. All code can be found on our GitHub.
