Nation and Expansion (1935–1938)

Hitler's regime adopted racial laws persecuting Jews and launched a program of territorial expansion. The Anschluss of Austria in 1938 expanded German territory without military resistance. Kristallnacht in November 1938 represented an escalation of state persecution of the Jewish population and signaled worse things to come.
